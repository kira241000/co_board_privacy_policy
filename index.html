1. Introduction

Welcome to CoBoard ("we," "our," or "us"). CoBoard is a shared whiteboard application that allows users to collaborate in real-time on digital whiteboards. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our mobile and web applications (collectively, the "Service"). By using CoBoard, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you create an account with CoBoard, we collect:

  • Email address: Used for authentication and account management
  • Password: Securely hashed and stored for authentication purposes
  • User ID: A unique identifier assigned to your account

2.2 Board and Drawing Data

To provide our collaborative whiteboard functionality, we collect and store:

  • Board content: All drawings, strokes, and annotations you create on whiteboards
  • Board metadata: Board names, creation dates, and modification timestamps
  • Collaboration data: Information about board members, permissions, and access levels
  • Invitation data: Email addresses and tokens used for board invitations

2.3 Usage Data

We may automatically collect certain information about how you use the Service:

  • Connection information: Connectivity status to ensure real-time synchronization
  • Device information: Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Local preferences: Theme settings (light/dark mode) and other app preferences stored locally on your device

2.4 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information about you from third-party authentication services (specifically Supabase) that you use to sign in to our Service.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our Service: Create and manage your account, enable board creation and collaboration
  • To enable real-time collaboration: Synchronize drawing data across all devices and users in real-time
  • To manage board sharing: Process invitations, manage board access, and coordinate permissions
  • To send notifications: Send you invitations to join boards and other service-related communications
  • To improve our Service: Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience and fix issues
  • To ensure security: Monitor for fraudulent activity, unauthorized access, and security threats
  • To comply with legal obligations: Meet legal requirements and respond to lawful requests

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a third-party cloud database service. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Board content, user information, and collaboration data are stored in Supabase's secure infrastructure.

Some preferences and settings may be stored locally on your device using secure local storage mechanisms.

4.2 Security Measures

We implement various security measures to protect your information:

  • Encrypted data transmission using secure protocols (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure password storage with industry-standard hashing algorithms
  • Access controls and authentication requirements
  • Regular security assessments and updates
Important: While we implement strong security measures, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we are committed to protecting your data using industry best practices.

5. Third-Party Services

CoBoard uses the following third-party services:

Service Purpose Data Processed
Supabase Backend-as-a-Service (authentication, database, real-time synchronization) User accounts, board data, drawing content, collaboration data

These third-party services have their own privacy policies governing how they collect, use, and protect your information. We encourage you to review Supabase's privacy policy at https://supabase.com/privacy.

Your use of these third-party services is subject to their terms of service and privacy policies. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

6.1 With Other Users

When you share a board or invite others to collaborate, the invited users will have access to:

  • The board's content and all drawings
  • Your email address (as shown to board members)
  • Information about your participation in the board

6.2 With Service Providers

We share your information with third-party service providers (such as Supabase) who perform services on our behalf. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and use it only for the purposes we specify.

6.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to:

  • Valid legal process, subpoenas, or court orders
  • Government requests
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users

6.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity, subject to the same privacy protections.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing where applicable
  • Opt-out: Opt out of certain data processing activities

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the "Contact Us" section below. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe.

Account Deletion: If you delete your account, all your boards and associated data will be permanently removed from our systems. However, content you shared with other users may remain accessible to them until they delete it.

8. Children's Privacy

CoBoard is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required by applicable law in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately, and we will take steps to delete such information.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Posting a notice in the app or on our website
  • Sending you an email notification (if you have provided your email address)

Your continued use of the Service after any changes to this Privacy Policy constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
